About my origin story.

Where I started. I've always been fascinated by human behavior—why we think, act, and interact the way we do.

What I studied. At Sam Houston State University (SHSU), I earned a degree in Communications, which sharpened my ability to analyze, synthesize, and convey complex information in a clear and impactful way. Studying communication theory, media, and rhetoric helped me develop strong storytelling and persuasion skills—both essential for advocating user needs in UX research. This background enables me to bridge the gap between data and design, translating research findings into actionable insights that drive decision-making and enhance user experiences. Building on this foundation, I pursued graduate studies in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at the University of Houston, where I deepened my expertise in research methodologies, usability testing, and design thinking. This advanced training allowed me to refine my approach to user-centered research, equipping me with the skills to optimize digital interactions, enhance workflows, and ensure that technology aligns seamlessly with human behavior. Together, my communication and HCI backgrounds provide a powerful combination of storytelling, analysis, and user advocacy, making me an effective bridge between users, designers, and decision-makers.

What drives me. I'm passionate about uncovering the “why” behind user behaviors—understanding their needs, frustrations, and motivations. Whether it’s developers navigating complex enterprise tools or end-users interacting with AI-driven experiences, my goal is to ensure that every product decision is informed by real-world insights, not assumptions.

What value I add. I bridge the gap between users and product teams, translating research into actionable strategies that drive product innovation. With a background spanning developer experience, AI-driven UX research, and enterprise software, I specialize in mixed-methods research, journey mapping, and usability testing to improve workflows, streamline onboarding, and enhance digital experiences. My work has led to measurable improvements, from boosting product adoption rates to optimizing user flows that reduce friction and improve engagement. Ultimately, I see UX research as more than just gathering data—it's about amplifying the user’s voice and ensuring technology serves people, not the other way around.

Principles my core values.

Human-Centered Design - Prioritize needs, remove friction, create intuitive experiences.

DesignOps - Combine qualitative and quantitative data for well-rounded insights.

Storytelling - Translate research findings into compelling narratives.
