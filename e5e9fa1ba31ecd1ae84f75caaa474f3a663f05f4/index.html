VTune Modernization and Multinode Analysis
Intel Optimization and Analysis Tools - VTune

August 2023

Overview

Partnered with product management to modernize VTune into a SaaS product, enhancing cloud-based multi-node performance profiling. The research focused on market demand, usability challenges, competitive landscape, and product improvement opportunities.

Key Contributions & Actions

  • Led a cross-functional research initiative, ensuring data-driven, actionable insights.
  • Conducted a 12-session usability study to identify key pain points: login issues, navigation inconsistencies, and workflow inefficiencies.
  • Mapped user mental models for multi-node performance analysis.
  • Evaluated competitors (Datadog, Azure Monitor, Grafana) and market trends in cloud profiling.
  • Identified key gaps: lack of ease of use, multi-node support, and automation.

Impact & Results

  • Influenced Intel’s cloud-based performance analysis strategy, improving usability, automation, and multi-node support.
  • Secured investment for additional research and iterative UX improvements.
  • Delivered a competitive landscape analysis, identifying innovation opportunities.
  • Provided data-backed insights to refine stakeholder assumptions and decision-making.
  • Shaped WTPK’s product roadmap, aligning with user needs.
  • Developed Jira-backed action items for seamless integration into the development cycle.
  • Presented research and secured funding and commitment from engineering for the creating and prototyping of conceptual designs in an interative fashion.

Through this initiative, I successfully aligned internal teams, validated market opportunities, and ensured research insights directly informed Intel’s next-generation cloud profiling tools.
